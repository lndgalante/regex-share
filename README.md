Regex Share
</h1>

## Preview

## Features

- Share your RegEx online in [RegExr](https://regexr.com)
- Generates example strings that matches your Regex

## How to

1. Select your RegEx

2) Press **CMD/CTRL + Shift + R**

3. Or press **CMD/CTRL + Shift + P** and find _Share your Regex: Share_
